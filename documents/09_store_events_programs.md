# Misty Jazz Records - Store Events & Programs

## Building Community Through Music

Our events celebrate jazz history, connect music lovers, and create unforgettable experiences. All events are free unless otherwise noted.

---

## Monthly Listening Sessions

### Last Friday Jazz Sessions

**When**: Last Friday of every month, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
**Where**: Misty Jazz Records main floor
**Capacity**: 30 people (RSVP recommended)
**Cost**: Free (donations accepted)

**What to Expect**:
- Curated album playback on high-end audio system
- Expert commentary and historical context
- Discussion and Q&A
- Complimentary refreshments
- Special pricing on featured albums

**Recent Themes**:
- Blue Note Records Golden Era (1955-1967)
- West Coast Cool Jazz Evolution
- John Coltrane's Spiritual Journey
- Jazz Vocals: Billie to Sarah
- Latin Jazz: Afro-Cuban Foundations

**RSVP**: events@mistyjazzrecords.com or (415) 555-5204

**Class Size**: 12 students maximum

**Topics Covered**:
- Vinyl anatomy and manufacturing
- Turntable basics and setup
- Record care and cleaning
- Grading and condition evaluation
- Building your collection
- Resources and community

**Materials Included**:
- Vinyl care kit
- Grading reference card
- Record cleaning cloth
- Discount voucher ($10 off next purchase)

## Event Policies

### General Rules
- **Respect**: Other attendees and staff
- **No Recording**: Without permission
- **Alcohol**: None served or allowed (all-ages events)
- **Food/Drink**: Provided only, no outside items
- **Capacity**: First-come, first-served unless RSVP
- **Photography**: Store photos OK, ask before photographing people

### RSVP/Cancellation
- **RSVP Deadline**: 48 hours before event
- **Cancellation**: 24 hours notice for paid events
- **No-Shows**: May affect future RSVP priority

### Accessibility
- **Wheelchair Accessible**: Main floor and restroom
- **ASL**: Available upon request (7 days notice)
- **Sensory**: Quiet room available
- **Service Animals**: Always welcome
