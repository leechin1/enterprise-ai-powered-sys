### Access & Correction
**You have the right to**:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Update your preferences
- Request data portability

**How to exercise**: Email privacy@mistyjazzrecords.com

### Marketing Communications
**You can opt-out of**:

- SMS notifications (reply STOP)
- Promotional emails

### European Residents (GDPR Rights)
**Additional rights**:
- Data portability

**To exercise**: Email privacy@mistyjazzrecords.com with subject "GDPR Request"
